Beginner Knee Rehab & Lower Body Program
3 days per week, 30-45 minutes per session

Week 1

Day 1: Lower Body A
Warm Up:
- Marching in place x 1 minute
- Ankle circles x 10 each direction
- Glute bridges x 12

Main Circuit: 3 sets
- Bodyweight squats x 10
- Step-ups (low box) x 8 each side
- Standing hip abduction x 12 each side

Core Stability: 2 sets
- Dead bug x 8 each side
- Bird dog x 8 each side

Cool Down:
- Quad stretch 30 sec each side
- Hamstring stretch 30 sec each side

Day 2: Balance & Core
Warm Up:
- Marching in place x 1 minute
- Cat-cow x 8

Main Circuit: 3 sets
- Single-leg balance x 30 sec each side
- Wall sit x 20 sec
- Clamshells x 12 each side

Core Stability: 2 sets
- Plank x 20 sec
- Side plank x 15 sec each side

Cool Down:
- Child's pose 30 sec
- Hip flexor stretch 30 sec each side

Day 3: Lower Body B
Warm Up:
- Marching in place x 1 minute
- Leg swings x 10 each direction

Main Circuit: 3 sets
- Sit-to-stand x 10
- Lateral band walks x 10 each side
- Calf raises x 15

Core Stability: 2 sets
- Dead bug x 8 each side
- Pelvic tilts x 12

Cool Down:
- Quad stretch 30 sec each side
- Calf stretch 30 sec each side

Week 2

(repeats the same 3 sessions, progressing reps or adding light resistance as tolerated)

Notes: patient reports mild anterior knee pain with stairs — avoid deep knee flexion, keep pain at or below 3/10 during exercise.
